写在开始.../At the beginning

2023-02-05 Views

Thoughts on this site

The original intention of this site was a bit chaotic. I wanted to create a daily updated visual interface for my previous projects on GitHub, but I had no experience with websites. At that time, I remembered that a friend around me was using Hexo for blogging, so I thought, “Ah, having a place to ramble on is not bad, and I can slowly experiment with how to put my programs online.” Thus, this site was born.
本站建立的初衷是有点混乱的， 因为本身是希望自己能把之前在github的项目做成一个可以每日更新的可视化界面， 但是奈何从未有过网站相关的经验。 这个时候想起身边有友人在用hexo做blog，于是自己就觉得 “啊，多一个自己胡说八道的地方也不错，正好也能慢慢试验着怎么把自己做过的程序放到网络上。” 于是就有了本站。

Update on 2025-05-28: I have built up the planned website with very limited functionalities, hope this tatty page could at least bring you a moment of pleasure.
更新于2025-05-28：当时构思的网站已经实现了些最基础的功能， 点进去看的话或许也可以博您一笑。

In regard of my content, I will try to write something related to weather, books, and my personal experiences. If there are any changes, I will update it here.
至于更新内容， 暂且会和书籍/气象/个人平时经历相关吧， 如若有什么变动的话会放上来的。

I am pretty lazy and this site does not have a comment section for now. Updates will depend upon my mood, but I will try to post something throughout the year.
我是个老鸽子了， 而且本站也暂时不设评论区， 更新全凭心情， 不过一年里多多少少会更些东西吧。
